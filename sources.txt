Data Fields

review_body: Text of the review provided by the customer. This is typically a 
free-form comment about the restaurant experience.
stars: An integer star rating (1-5) given by the customer to the restaurant, representing their satisfaction level.

Additional Information

The dataset was collected in 2025 from the Wongnai platform and is available for research and analysis purposes. 
It contains a diverse set of reviews from different types of restaurants, making it suitable for a variety of tasks, 
including sentiment analysis and review recommendation systems.
